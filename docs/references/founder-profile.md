# Founder Story

## Why Ignite The Soul Exists

Ignite The Soul was created from Mafaza's passion for helping women reconnect with themselves through meaningful conversations, shared experiences, and supportive communities.

Rather than offering traditional coaching alone, the vision is to create spaces where women feel emotionally safe, heard, understood, and encouraged to grow together.

This story should always be told authentically and in Mafaza's own voice.
