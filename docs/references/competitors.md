# Competitive Landscape

## Purpose

This document captures research into brands operating within the women's wellness, coaching, retreat, and personal development space.

Its purpose is to identify opportunities for differentiation, improve positioning, and ensure Ignite The Soul develops its own authentic identity rather than following industry trends.

This document is for internal strategy only.

---

# Research Goals

The research should answer:

- Who serves a similar audience?
- How do they communicate?
- What products do they offer?
- What visual identity do they use?
- What emotional experience do they create?
- What opportunities exist for Ignite The Soul?

---

# Competitor Categories

## Direct Competitors

Brands offering similar services.

Examples:

- Women's coaching
- Women's circles
- Wellness retreats
- Personal development communities

---

## Indirect Competitors

Brands competing for attention.

Examples:

- Meditation platforms
- Wellness apps
- Therapy platforms
- Lifestyle communities

---

## Aspirational Brands

Brands admired for quality rather than service overlap.

These help inspire positioning rather than product strategy.
