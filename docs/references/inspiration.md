# Inspiration & References

## Purpose

This document collects visual, interaction, UX, and storytelling references that inspire the Ignite The Soul website.

These references guide design decisions throughout the project but should never be copied directly.

The goal is inspiration, not imitation.

---

# Design Direction

The desired experience can be summarized as:

> A calm, editorial, nature-inspired digital sanctuary that feels warm, spacious, timeless, and deeply human.

Visitors should feel they have entered a peaceful space rather than a commercial website.

# Photography Direction

Photography should feel:

- Natural
- Genuine
- Calm
- Emotional
- Documentary

Prefer:

- Real women
- Workshops
- Conversations
- Nature
- Outdoor light
- Community

Avoid:

- Stock imagery
- Fake smiles
- Corporate portraits
- Over-editing

---

# Motion Inspiration

GSAP + Lenis

Motion should feel almost invisible.

Preferred interactions

- Gentle fade-ins
- Soft upward movement
- Smooth scrolling
- Slow image reveals
- Elegant hover states

Avoid

- Bounce
- Spin
- Heavy parallax
- Flashy transitions
- Oversized page transitions

---

# What To Avoid

Avoid websites that feel:

- Corporate
- Sales driven
- Busy
- Template based
- Over animated
- Colourful for the sake of attention
- Filled with marketing jargon

Specifically avoid:

- Countdown timers
- Popups
- Auto-playing videos with sound
- Aggressive CTAs
- Feature comparison tables
- Generic wellness templates
