# About Ignite The Soul

Ignite The Soul is a women's wellness brand founded by Mafaza Rafeek.

The brand exists to create a safe, supportive, and inspiring environment where women can reconnect with themselves through meaningful experiences, coaching, community, and personal growth.

Rather than selling products, Ignite The Soul offers transformational experiences that help women slow down, reflect, heal, and grow.

The website should reflect this philosophy in every interaction.

Visitors should never feel like they are browsing a corporate business website.

Instead, they should feel as though they have entered a peaceful sanctuary where they are welcomed, understood, and gently guided.

---

# Vision

To become one of Sri Lanka's most trusted women's wellness communities by creating meaningful experiences that inspire confidence, resilience, healing, and authentic personal growth.

---

# Mission

To help women reconnect with themselves through coaching, workshops, retreats, women's circles, and community while providing practical guidance, emotional support, and safe spaces for growth.

---

# Brand Promise

Every woman who visits Ignite The Soul should feel:

- Safe
- Calm
- Welcome
- Supported
- Inspired
- Hopeful

The website should create these emotions before asking visitors to register for any offering.

# Project Principles

Every design and development decision should follow these principles.

## Human First

Design for people, not trends.

## Calm Over Complexity

Keep interfaces simple, spacious, and effortless.

## Editorial Over Template

Avoid generic layouts.

Design with thoughtful composition and storytelling.

## Quality Over Quantity

Every section should have purpose.

Nothing unnecessary should exist.

## Emotional Before Functional

The website should create a feeling before asking users to take action.
